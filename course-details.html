Course Details:

This course covers the fundamental concepts of biological sciences, including cell biology, genetics, and ecology. Students will engage in hands-on laboratory work and field studies to enhance their understanding of living organisms and their interactions with the environment.

Course Syllabus

  • Requirements
  • Fees
  • Student Profile
  • How to Apply

Requirements

Students should have completed their high school education with a focus on science subjects. A passion for learning about living organisms is essential.

Fees

The total course fee is ₹50,000, which can be paid in installments. Scholarships are available for eligible students.

Student Profile

Students will be expected to participate actively in class discussions, laboratory work, and group projects. A strong commitment to academic integrity is required.

How to Apply

Interested students can apply online through our website or visit our campus for more information. Applications are accepted year-round.

A typical weekly timetable:

Classes are held from Monday to Friday, with practical sessions scheduled in the laboratory and field trips planned throughout the semester.

  • 1st Year

    First Year Courses

    • Introduction to Biology
    • Cell Biology
    • Genetics
    • Ecology

    Assessment

    • Mid-term exams and final exams will assess students' understanding of the material.
    • Practical assessments will also be conducted in the laboratory.
  • 2nd Year

    Second Year Courses

    • Microbiology
    • Biochemistry
    • Botany
    • Zoology

    Assessment

    • Students will complete projects and presentations as part of their assessment.
  • 3rd Year

    Third Year Courses

    • Advanced Genetics
    • Environmental Science
    • Research Methodology

    Assessment

    • Students will submit a research project and participate in a final examination.
